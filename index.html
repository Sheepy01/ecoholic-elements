How It Works

Six Simple Steps to Zero Waste

1

Install Smart Bins

We deploy QR-coded segregation bins at your facility with color-coded categories for organic, plastic, paper, and metal waste.

2

Scheduled Pickups

Our collection vehicles arrive on schedule. Drivers scan QR codes to log pickup time, weight, and waste categories via mobile app.

3

Waste Segregation

At our facility, trained workers further segregate materials using conveyor systems and quality control processes.

4

Process at Facility

Organic waste goes to composting. Plastics are cleaned and shredded. Paper is baled. Metals are sorted and sold.

5

Sell Recycled Products

We sell compost, plastic flakes, recycled materials, and bio-feedstock to manufacturers and agricultural markets.

6

Generate Reports

Clients receive monthly dashboards with waste diversion rates, carbon credits earned, and EPR compliance certificates.
